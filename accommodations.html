Accommodations

Graduating students received instructions on how to request on-campus dormitory accommodations for their Graduation guests. Each student can request up to four beds.



Arrival Instructions

Guests staying in dormitories must complete a security screening at the Administration Building before preceding to the check-in table at the River Overlook tent.


At check-in, guests will receive a keycard which will grant them access to the building and room they are assigned. The keycard also grants access to Campus Dining meals. Guests can then proceed to their assigned room.



Other Options

Limited accommodations are available off-campus at the Scholar Inn. Nearby municipalities may also have available accommodations.
