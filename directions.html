Directions

There are many ways to get to Auburn, and the Academy is often a quick Tube ride away.


Auxilliary entrances to the Academy near venues will be selectively opened thirty minutes prior to and following graduation events. Outside of those hours, all visitors must enter campus through the Administration Building.



MRT

Guests can take the MRT Southern Line to Auburn Station (SW20). The Academy is just outside the station.



Third-party Rail

NewRail FLR offers service to Auburn Station on Line R1. Access to the Academy is through the MRT station.



Ferry

IntraSail serves the Auburn Marina on line 1. The Academy will run shuttles from the marina during Graduation events.



Air

Many airlines serve Auburn Airport (ABN). The Academy will run shuttles from the airport during Graduation events.
